KERALA

Kerala is a beautiful state of India which is also known as “Godson on Country”. Which is located on the southwestern border of India between the Arabian Sea and the Sahadri mountain ranges. Kerala is surrounded by seas on one side and Karnataka and Tamil Nadu states on the other side. Its capital is Thiruvananthapuram (Trivandrum).The language spoken here is Malayalam. The area of ​​this state is 38,863 square kilometers.
